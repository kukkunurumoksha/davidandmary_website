Cambridge Early Years Curriculum

The Cambridge Early Years curriculum is designed to develop the social and cognitive abilities of young learners through exploration, discovery, and play.

Our programs allow children to develop confidence, responsibility, reflection, innovation, and engagement with the world around them.

Our Teaching Approach

At David & Mary Academy, we implement the Cambridge curriculum through a dynamic approach that puts each child's individual learning style at the forefront.

1

Inquiry-Based Learning

Children are encouraged to ask questions, explore, and discover answers through hands-on experiences.

2

Differentiated Instruction

We recognize that each child learns differently and tailor our teachings to meet their unique needs.

3

Thematic Learning

Our curriculum is organized around engaging themes that integrate subjects across multiple disciplines.

Assessment and Progress Monitoring

We take a holistic approach to assessment, ensuring that every child's progress is monitored in a way that supports their development.

Observations About Assessment

Teachers carefully observe children's learning in both structured and unstructured environments.

Learning Portfolios

Each child has a portfolio documenting their work, discoveries, and developmental milestones.

Progress Reports

Detailed reports show children's growth across learning areas, highlighting accomplishments and areas for growth.

Parent-Teacher Conferences

Regular meetings provide parents with insights into their child's educational journey.

Admission Process

1

Inquiry and Tour

Contact us to schedule a campus tour where you and your children can experience the campus environment first-hand.

2

Application Submission

Complete and submit the application form along with the required documents and application fee.

3

Assessment and Interview

Children participate in a gentle assessment and a child's interview to determine their developmental stage.

4

Admission Offer

Successful applicants will be notified of the admission offer, and payment details will be provided to your family.

5

Enrollment and Orientation

Secure your child's place by submitting enrollment documents, fees, and attending orientation.

Required Documents

For Application

  • ● Completed application form
  • ● Birth certificate (copy of child's)
  • ● Passport-sized photographs (4)
  • ● Record of health or proof of immunization
  • ● Proof of address (utility bill/government ID)
  • ● Non-refundable application fee

For Enrollment (After Acceptance)

  • ● Signed enrollment contract
  • ● Medical clearance form (child's health)
  • ● Emergency contact information
  • ● Parent consent form (field trips/photographs)
  • ● Transportation form (if applicable)
  • ● Registration fee and term tuition payment
