What we Offer

At David & Mary Academy, we provide a nurturing environment where your child will thrive academically, socially, and emotionally.

Experienced & Caring Professionals

Our dedicated teachers bring years of experience in early childhood education, combining expertise with genuine care for every child's wellbeing and development.

Blended Curriculum

Our internationally recognized blended curriculum promotes holistic development, combining the best of structured learning with creative exploration for well-rounded academic excellence.

Child-Centered Approach

We place your child at the heart of everything we do, tailoring our teaching methods to suit each individual's learning style, interests, and developmental pace.

Enrichment & Play-Based Activities

Music, art, physical education, and hands-on play activities develop well-rounded individuals, fostering creativity, curiosity, and a lifelong love of learning.

Progress Tracking

Regular assessments and detailed progress reports keep parents informed about their child's growth, ensuring every milestone is celebrated and every challenge is addressed.

Fostering Potential

We believe every child is uniquely gifted. Our educators are trained to identify and nurture each child's individual talents, helping them stand out and shine in their own way.

Safety & Hygiene

We provide a safe, clean, and comfortable space where children feel secure and happy. Our facilities are designed with child safety and hygiene as top priorities.

Prompt Updates

Regular communication and timely updates keep parents informed about their child's daily activities, progress, and any important school announcements.
