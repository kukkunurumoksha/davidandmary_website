About Us

David & Mary Academy is an outstanding preschool dedicated to providing a nurturing environment where young minds can flourish and grow in excellence.

Each Life Is a precious gift, deserving of care and celebration. Life holds an incredible potential to expand and blossom in myriad directions, shaping futures and building legacies, enriched by the choices we make and the values we uphold.

What We Are: Our institution was founded with a heartfelt vision to reach the young hearts entrusted to us with love and to guide them in discovering the true essence of life. In an era where good morals are often sidelined, we dedicate ourselves to instilling timeless values and principles, apart from academic knowledge.

What We Do: At our core, we are unwavering in our commitment to nurturing each child's inherent Curiosity, Creativity, and Character. Our nurturing environment is carefully designed to support and guide them on their journey to becoming Confident, Courageous, Competent, and Compassionate individuals.

What We Aim: Through our dedicated efforts, we aim to inspire and empower young minds to flourish, encouraging them to embrace their unique potential and contribute positively to the world around them. Together, we can cultivate a future where kindness, integrity, and empathy thrive.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, nurturing, and engaging early learning environment where each child is lovingly cared for and is encouraged to grow in wisdom, strength, and character.

We are committed to supporting their overall — social, intellectual, physical, & emotional development — equipping them to fulfill God's purpose for their lives and walk in faith, hope, and love.

Our Vision

To nurture each child's natural curiosity and desire to explore, guiding them with love and wisdom toward their God-given purpose.

We strive to inspire children to pursue their dreams with Courage, Character, and Creativity — raising up a new generation of leaders.

We have examples of David and Mary who in humility and obedience were prepared for a greater purpose in their lives, thereby making a meaningful impact in the world!
