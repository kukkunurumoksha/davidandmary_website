About Us

David & Mary Academy is an outstanding preschool dedicated to providing a nurturing environment where young minds can flourish and grow in the spirit of excellence.

Our Story

Founded with a vision to provide exceptional early childhood education, David & Mary Academy has been nurturing young minds since its inception. Our journey began with a simple belief: every child deserves a strong educational foundation.

Today, we stand as a beacon of educational excellence, offering the prestigious Blended curriculum to children aged 2.5 to 6 years, preparing them for future academic success while fostering creativity and critical thinking.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, nurturing, and engaging early learning environment where each child is lovingly cared for and is encouraged to grow in wisdom, strength, and character.

We are committed to supporting their overall — social, intellectual, physical, & emotional development — equipping them to fulfill God's purpose for their lives and walk in faith, hope, and love.

Our Vision

To nurture each child's natural curiosity and desire to explore, guiding them with love and wisdom toward their God-given purpose.

We strive to inspire children to pursue their dreams with Courage, Character, and Creativity — raising up a new generation of leaders.

We have examples of David and Mary who in humility and obedience were prepared for a greater purpose in their lives, thereby making a meaningful impact in the world!
