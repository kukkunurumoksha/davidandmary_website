About Us

David & Mary Academy is a premier preschool dedicated to providing a nurturing environment where young minds can flourish and grow.

Our Story

Founded with a vision to provide exceptional early childhood education, David & Mary Academy has been nurturing young minds since its inception. Our journey began with a simple belief: every child deserves a strong educational foundation.

Today, we stand as a beacon of educational excellence, offering the prestigious Blended curriculum to children aged 2.5 to 6 years, preparing them for future academic success while fostering creativity and critical thinking.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, nurturing, and engaging early learning environment where each child is lovingly cared for and is encouraged to grow in wisdom, strength, and character. We are committed to supporting their overall — social, intellectual, physical, & emotional development — equipping them to fulfill God's purpose for their lives and walk in faith, hope, and love.

Our Vision

To nurture each child's natural curiosity and desire to explore, guiding them with love and wisdom toward their God-given purpose. We strive to inspire children to pursue their dreams with courage, character, and creativity—raising up a new generation of leaders with bold hearts like David and humility like Mary, prepared to make a meaningful impact in the world.

Our Approach

At David & Mary Academy, we believe in a holistic approach to education that nurtures all aspects of a child's development - cognitive, physical, social, and emotional.

  • Child-centered learning environments
  • Play-based learning with structured activities
  • Low student-teacher ratio for personalized attention
  • Regular parent-teacher communication
